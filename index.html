About Me

My relationship with exercise and “fitness” began at twenty, after I was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes. That same week, I went on my first run—just down the driveway and back—and started a simple bodyweight strength routine. I run a bit farther now, but living with a chronic illness has taught me this: the best exercise is the kind you can take anywhere, do consistently, and that truly supports your overall health.

For the past eight years as a functional trainer and licensed massage therapist I’ve helped more than 3,000 people recover from injuries, surgeries, and chronic pain. My programs:

  • Offer simple, effective exercise routines that fit into everyday life
  • Balance training and recovery for steady, sustainable progress
  • Support mobility, prehab/rehab, stress management, and better sleep

I’m here to partner with you and help you find your own rhythm and relationship with movement.
